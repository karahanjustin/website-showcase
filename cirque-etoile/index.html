Welcome to Cirque Étoile

Where imagination meets reality.

Located in the heart of Cologne, Cirque Étoile is more than just a circus. It is a permanent entertainment destination that combines breathtaking performances with a vibrant leisure experience. Here, art, movement, music, and emotion come together to create unforgettable moments for visitors of all ages.

Our circus is proudly animal-free, focusing entirely on the incredible talents of human performers. From acrobatics and aerial artistry to dance and visual storytelling, every show is designed to inspire and amaze.

Step into a world of lights, stars, and endless wonder — welcome to Cirque Étoile.

Our Stars
Aerial Artist
Luna Moreau

Luna is our queen of the skies. With grace and strength, she performs breathtaking aerial silk routines high above the stage. Her performances tell emotional stories through movement, leaving the audience in awe every night.

Acrobat
Mateo Silva

Mateo is known for his explosive energy and fearless acrobatics. Whether it's flips, jumps, or complex group routines, he brings power and excitement to every performance.

Contortionist
Aria Novak

Aria pushes the limits of the human body. Her elegant and mesmerizing contortion acts combine flexibility, control, and artistic expression in a truly unique way.

Juggler
Leo Zhang

Leo transforms juggling into an art form. With perfect precision and creative choreography, he creates fast-paced, visually stunning performances that captivate the audience.

Clown & Performer
Nico Rossi

Nico brings laughter and charm to Cirque Étoile. With a mix of comedy, improvisation, and audience interaction, he creates unforgettable moments full of joy.
